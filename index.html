From Teacher to People's Leader

A Journey of Service and Dedication

Born on July 16, 1977, in Sangphur village, Tuensang, Nagaland, S. Keoshu Yimchunger's story is one of perseverance and unwavering commitment to public service. From working in a hotel to support his studies to becoming a respected educator and eventually an MLA, his journey exemplifies the power of determination.

Key Milestones

  • Born: July 16, 1977, Sangphur village, Tuensang
  • Education: Self-funded through hotel work
  • Career: 24 years as government teacher
  • Political Entry: Elected MLA unopposed (2021)
  • Re-election: Successfully re-elected (2023)
  • Current Role: Advisor to CM - Youth Resources & Sports

His transition from education to politics was driven by a deep desire to create systemic change and provide better opportunities for the youth and rural communities of Nagaland.

Milestones That Matter

Unopposed MLA Victory

Elected as MLA of Shamator–Chessore constituency unopposed in 2021, demonstrating widespread community trust and support.

Youth & Sports Advisor

Currently serving as Advisor to Chief Minister for Youth Resources & Sports (2023–Present), focusing on youth empowerment initiatives.

24 Years in Education

Dedicated over two decades to education as a government teacher, shaping young minds and building foundations for future leaders.

Information & PR Advisor

Former Advisor for Information & Public Relations, enhancing government communication and transparency initiatives.

Community Champion

Recognized community leader known for helping the poor and supporting educational access for underprivileged children.

Re-elected 2023

Successfully re-elected in 2023, reaffirming the people's confidence in his leadership and vision for Nagaland's development.
